Built by Dr. Adam Bell

Dr. Adam Bell is a Texas educator and school administrator who understands the daily pressure of campus operations: hallway accountability, student movement, teacher workflow, school safety, and protecting instructional time. HallPass was built from that reality. It is not a product imagined in a conference room; it is a practical system designed around the problems campus leaders and teachers deal with every day. The goal is simple: give schools better visibility, cleaner routines, and a lower-cost way to manage student movement without adding another complicated platform to the school day.

Google-based setup

HallPass is built around Google Apps Script and Google Sheets, making it easier for many schools to test, configure, and manage without standing up a separate enterprise system.
