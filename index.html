About Dr. Adam Bell

Dr. Adam Bell is an educator and school administrator with firsthand experience in campus operations, student accountability, school safety, teacher support, and instructional leadership. HallPass was built from a simple belief: schools need systems that help adults do their jobs better without making the school day more complicated.

Privacy and school data

The public HallPass demo uses fake student data only. No real student information is required to test the software. Campus implementation should be reviewed with district technology, legal, and student data privacy expectations before launch.
