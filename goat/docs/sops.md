# Goat Bot SOPs

## Buying goats

1. Buy in batches of 5.
2. Enter expected weight, price, market, and sale target before buying.
3. Approve the purchase only if the floor case is profitable as a #2 goat.
4. Do not buy when active inventory is 35+ unless sale goats are already staged.
5. Do not buy when Bay 1 and Bay 2 are both occupied.
6. Do not buy when Bay 3 has 3+ recovery/problem goats.
7. Do not buy when overdue tasks are out of control.

## Intake day

1. Prepare Bay 1 or Bay 2 before unloading.
2. Tag each goat.
3. Weigh each goat.
4. Record purchase price, source, sex, and intake weight.
5. Assume quality = #2.
6. Create the first weigh task and first health exam task.
7. Start 14-day quarantine.
8. Do not mix intake goats with older goats.

## Quarantine

- Days 1-14 are quarantine.
- Watch appetite, stool, cough, limp, and alertness daily.
- Record all medicine.
- Any goat with a withdrawal period must be blocked from sale until cleared.
- Healthy goats can move to Bay 4 or Bay 5 after quarantine.

## Daily routine

1. Feed.
2. Check water.
3. Observe each bay.
4. Check health alerts.
5. Complete medicine follow-up tasks.
6. Check sale staging.
7. Mark tasks complete.

## Weekly routine

1. Weigh goats due for weigh-in.
2. Complete FAMACHA/body condition checks.
3. Clean/check pens.
4. Review feed, hay, mineral, and medicine inventory.
5. Review floor-profit forecast.
6. Decide sell/hold for sale-ready goats.

## Pen movement

- Bay 1-2: quarantine.
- Bay 3: recovery/problem/withdrawal.
- Bay 4-5: starter grow-out.
- Bay 6: mid grow-out.
- Bay 7: finish.
- Bay 8: sale staging.
- Record every movement.

## Sale day

1. Confirm no sale goat is in withdrawal.
2. Confirm target weight and market signal.
3. Stage goats in Bay 8.
4. Record sale weight, quality, gross/net sale, and fees.
5. Close out actual profit per goat and batch.

## Profit rule

If the goat has to become #1 to make money, it is not a Goat Bot buy.
